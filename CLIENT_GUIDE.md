# 🎂 Mini Crumbs: Owner's Management Guide

Welcome to your new bakery management system! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to manage your products and orders like a pro.

---

## 🔐 1. Accessing Your Dashboard
To manage your bakery, you need to log in to the private Admin Dashboard.

1.  **Go to your website URL** and add `/dashboard` at the end (e.g., `www.minicrumbs.com/dashboard`).
2.  **Login**: Use your admin email and password to sign in.
3.  **Home Screen**: You will see your "Overview" with total revenue and pending order counts.

---

## 🧁 2. Managing Your Menu (Products)
You can find all your products in the **"My Products"** tab.

### ➕ How to Add a New Product
1.  Click **"Add Product"** in the sidebar.
2.  **Name & Category**: Give your product a name and select its category (e.g., Skillet Cakes).
3.  **Image**: Click the upload box to select a photo of the product. High-quality photos sell better!
4.  **Description**: Write a short, delicious description.
5.  **Save**: Click **"Publish to Menu"**.

### 📏 How to Add Product Variants (Sizes & Prices)
Products can have different sizes with different prices.
1.  In the "Add Product" form, find the **"Product Variants"** section.
2.  Click **"Add Variant"**.
3.  Enter the **Size** (e.g., `1kg`) and the **Price** (e.g., `1200`).
4.  You can add as many as you need (e.g., 1/2kg, 1kg, 2kg).

### ❌ How to Delete a Product
1.  Go to the **"My Products"** tab.
2.  Find the product you want to remove and click the **Delete (Trash can)** icon.
3.  Confirm the deletion. *Warning: This cannot be undone!*

### 🚫 How to make a Product "Out of Stock"
If you run out of ingredients but don't want to delete the product:
1.  Go to **"My Products"**.
2.  look at the product u wanna make out of stock
3. click the eye icon to make it out of stock (or)
4.  Click the **Edit (Pencil)** icon on the product.
5.  Change the **Availability** dropdown to **"Out of Stock"**.
6.  Save changes. The product will now show as "Sold Out" to customers.

---

## 📋 3. Managing Standard Orders
Click the **"Order List"** tab to see all orders coming from your menu.

### 💳 How to View Payment Screenshot
Since customers must pay before finishing checkout:
1.  Find the order card in the list.
2.  Look for the **"Payment"** section on the card.
3.  Click **"View Screenshot"**. This will open the customer's payment proof in a new tab.

### 🔄 Using the Order Status Buttons (Auto message)
You can update the progress of an order using the buttons at the bottom of each card:
each button you click will open in whatsapp and send message to the customer 
*   **Checkmark (Green)**: Click this to move the order to the next stage (e.g., from *Pending* to *Received*, then *Baking*, then *Delivered*).
*   **X (Red)**: Use this only if you need to cancel an order.

---

## 🌟 4. Managing Custom Orders
Click the **"Custom Orders"** tab to see special cake requests.

*   These are requests where customers want something unique.
*   **Reference Image**: Click the image icon on the card to see what design the customer wants.
*   **WhatsApp Chat**: Click the phone number to quickly message the customer to discuss pricing or details.

---

## 🛒 5. The Customer Checkout Flow
Here is what your customers experience when they buy from you:

1.  **Browse**: Customer adds delicious cakes to their cart.
2.  **Order Summary**: They click Checkout and review their items.
3.  **Payment**: They are shown a **QR Code** for the exact total. They scan and pay via GPay/PhonePe.
4.  **Proof**: They **must upload a screenshot** of the payment to proceed.
5.  **Details**: They fill in their name, phone, and delivery address.
6.  **WhatsApp**: They click "Confirm" and are sent to **WhatsApp** to send you the final order message. 

*You will receive a WhatsApp message with all the details AND a link to their payment screenshot!*

---

**Tip**: The Dashboard automatically refreshes every 30 seconds, so you'll see new orders pop up without having to reload the page!
